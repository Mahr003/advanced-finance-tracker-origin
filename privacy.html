Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

Advanced Finance Tracker ("the App") is a client-side web application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

The App collects only the data you explicitly enter:

  • Transaction titles, amounts, categories, and dates
  • Your theme preference (light or dark mode)
  • Your language preference (English or Chinese)
  • Your cookie consent choice

3. How Data Is Stored

All data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. No data is transmitted to any server, third party, or external service. The App has no back-end and makes no network requests on your behalf.

4. Cookies and localStorage

We use browser localStorage (not HTTP cookies) to persist your transactions and preferences between sessions. This storage is local to your device and browser. Clearing your browser data will permanently delete all stored information.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No third-party scripts are loaded.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All data is visible within the App at all times.
  • Export: Use the "Export CSV" feature to download your data.
  • Deletion: Clear your browser's localStorage for this site to erase all data permanently.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. It is intended for general personal finance management by adults.

8. Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
